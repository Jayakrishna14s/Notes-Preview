How the APP Works

SharedPreferences

The app uses SharedPreferences to store user data locally on the device. This ensures that the user's notes remain accessible even after the app is closed or the device is restarted. SharedPreferences allows for easy saving and retrieval of key-value pairs, providing a lightweight and efficient way to store small amounts of data such as notes.

Operations (CRUD)

Create

The app allows users to create new notes through a simple interface, entering text and saving it for future reference.

Read

Users can view their saved notes by scrolling through a list. Each note is displayed with its title and a preview of the content.

Update

Notes can be edited, allowing users to update the text, title, or any other details of their existing notes.

Delete

Notes can be deleted at any time. Once deleted, the note is permanently removed from the app, ensuring that users have full control over their data.
